Meet Carly Ballenger

LICENSED ESTHETICIAN CERTIFIED ACNE SPECIALIST

Emmy Runyon

LICENSED ESTHETICIAN CERTIFIED ACNE SPECIALIST

Emmy is a Licensed Esthetician with five years of industry experience. Her passion for treating acne was born from her own personal skin struggles. After countless trips to the dermatologist, where she was told Accutane was the only option she had left, she decided to take things into her own hands. She began seeing an esthetician who took an entirely different approach to treating her acne, and that’s when she realized she wanted to do for others what her esthetician had done for her.

After graduating from the Aveda Institute and spending several years working at Columbus-area spas, she decided to specialize in treating acne, which is how she ended up joining the team at Skinspired. Emmy’s favorite part about treating acne is sitting down with new clients for a consultation and getting to know them, and their skin journey, on a deeper level.

Rachel Leavitt

LICENSED ESTHETICIAN CERTIFIED ACNE SPECIALIST

Rachel is a licensed esthetician that comes to Skinspired Acne Clinic with 20 years of customer service experience. Prior to joining the clinic, she spent 10 years working night shift with moms and babies at the hospital. After her own personal struggle with acne, she decided to go to school and get her esthetic license to help others with their own acne struggle. She is passionate about helping others look and feel like the best version of themselves. While she loves helping others clear their skin she also loves to give a good lash lift & tint!

When Rachel is not at the clinic you can find her spending time with her husband and 2 boys, mostly at the ball fields.
